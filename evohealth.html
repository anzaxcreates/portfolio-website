EvoHealth

Let me introduce you to EvoHealth, a digital wellness application providing insight into improving physical and mental health.

Project Objective

My main objective and inspiration to create EvoHealth is to create a tool that will allow users to improve their mental health with adapting habits that can help them adopt a healthy lifestyle.

My Contributions

  • Company: Career Foundry UX Design Case Study
  • MyRole: UX Researcher, UX Designer, UI Designer
  • Timeline: July 2021 - February 2022
  • Tools: Figma, InVision, Miro, and Optimal Sort

My Approach

Problem Statement

Users need an inclusive and intuitive to help with high stress and anxiety as it is effecting their daily life and routine personally and professionally. We will know this when they consistently engage on the application and utilize the features.

User Research

For my User Research, I conducted user interviews.

Research Goals

  • What kind of problems users have maintaining and enhancing a health and wellness lifestyle?
  • Document user opinions on if they track their moods, behaviors, and thoughts throughout the day.
  • What specific featurers would they like to see and what features they don't like to help better our design?

Low-Fidelity Wireframes

This low fidelity wireframe is the first translation of my previous research. The idea is to focus on the core features and the big picture of the project without spending time on small details (visuals, spacing, colors etc.). Hand drawn wireframes are a great communication tool to address to the team, stakeholders and to potential users.
